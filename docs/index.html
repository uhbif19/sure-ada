Sure Ada. Make your portfolio free of market and security risks

Our project is fully decentralized parametric system, with insurance premium determined by prediction market.

Our solution would consist of three parts:

  1. Fully automated logic recording if insurable events happened on-chain
  2. Open prediction market, determining probability of insured event happening
  3. Insurance module providing insurance by current market evaluation + fee from independent insuring providers

Such design would be completely open both for users and investors. It would be independent from centralized claim evaluators and risk estimation models, that users should believe in.
